Mission & Vision

MediQuills, founded by Ridhi Gutta, is dedicated to increasing medical literacy in children. By creating engaging, informative, and accessible books, we strive to reach as many children as we can. We aim to inspire young minds to understand, appreciate, and actively participate in their health and well-being from an early age. Through our captivating content, we aspire to cultivate a generation of medically literate individuals who are curious, informed, and proactive about their health.

Series

How Does It Work: A Series of Medical Books For Children

How Does It Work: A Series of Medical Books For Children is a series meant to educate children about various topics in medicine and improve youth medical literacy. This engaging and educational series takes young readers on an exciting journey through the wonders of the human body and the amazing world of medicine. Perfect for curious minds aged 6-12, each book in this series answers common questions kids have about their bodies and how they function, using simple language and vivid illustrations.

How Does It Work: Breaking Medical Myths in Children

From Book 1: This book explains vaccines in a simple and engaging way for young readers. It covers what vaccines are, how they help the immune system fight off germs, and why they’re important for keeping everyone healthy. The book also discusses the different types of vaccines, how they are made, and why some require multiple doses. Through easy-to-understand explanations and fun illustrations, the book aims to educate kids on the importance of vaccines in protecting themselves and others!
