How Does It Work: A Series of Medical Books For Children

This is a series meant to educate children about various topics in medicine and improve youth medical literacy. This engaging and educational series takes young readers on an exciting journey through the wonders of the human body and the amazing world of medicine. Perfect for curious minds aged 6-12, each book in this series answers common questions kids have about their bodies and how they function, using simple language and vivid illustrations.
